Cycle the Continental Divide

Dates: Jul 12, 2018 – Jul 18, 2018

Route: We’ll travel from the lively resort town of Whitefish, located near Glacier National Park, to the southern end of the spectacular Swan Range, one of Montana’s less-visited geologic gems. Not far away rests Flathead Lake, the largest natural freshwater lake in the West. Riding mostly on abandoned logging roads and a bit of single-track trail, we’ll experience the high country of one of the world’s great wild-land complexes.

Cost: $1,159.00

Basic requirements: Enthusiasm, Energy, Intermediate/Advanced experience cycling, bike and cycling equipment.
